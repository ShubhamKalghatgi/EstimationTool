BMS — AHU Control Architecture
BMS Cost Estimation Methodology (AHU): Based on ASHRAE 90.1 + ASHRAE Guideline 36 + Comapny product catalogue. Costs derived from: (1) Equipment cost (material + procurement), (2) Installation labour (mechanical + electrical + commissioning), (3) Engineering overhead (15% of equipment), (4) Contingency (10%), (5) Integration & testing (BACnet/EcoStruxure). Labour rates: ₹600–1200/hr (BMS technician), ₹400–700/hr (electrician). Prices in INR, India market 2024–25.

BMS — VAV Zone Control Schematic
VAV Cost Methodology: Zone-based costing per ASHRAE Guideline 36 VAV sequences. Each zone: VAV box + actuator + zone controller + room sensor + wiring loom. Key parameters: number of zones, pressure-independent (PIBCV) vs pressure-dependent boxes, reheat type, sensor sophistication. Schneider pricing based on SpaceLogic VAV controllers + SE8000/SE8600 room sensors + AS-B network controllers.

System Diagram — Fan Coil Unit
BMS — FCU Control Architecture
FCU Cost Methodology: Room-by-room unit costing. Key parameters: pipe configuration (2-pipe seasonal / 4-pipe simultaneous), fan speed control (3-speed relay vs EC motor), valve type (2-way/3-way), controller model. Schneider's SE8000 series handles fan speed, valve position, setpoint & scheduling. Labour-intensive due to individual room access; BMS allocation typically 35–40% equipment, 40–45% labour, 15–20% engineering.

Methodology & Standards Applied
BMS Cost Estimation Standards
ASHRAE Guideline 36 High-performance sequences of operation for HVAC systems. Equipment sizing and control sequences applied for AHU, VAV, and FCU systems.
CIBSE / RS Means Labour hour benchmarks from CIBSE commissioning guides. Regional India multiplier applied to international base rates.
Engineering Overhead (15%) Covers: design drawings, loop diagrams, points schedule, DDC programming, submittals, O&M manuals, project management.
Contingency (10%) Applied to subtotal for unforeseen site conditions, scope creep, integration complications, and retesting.
Schneider EcoStruxure SpaceLogic AS-P / AS-B controllers, SE8000 / SE8650 / SE9000 room sensors, ATV VFD drives, SXWAS40QBXX BACnet/IP gateway, VXT/VVF modulating valves.

Disclaimer
Estimates are indicative based on Schneider Electric India catalogue pricing (2024–25) and standard BMS industry labour norms. Actual costs will vary based on site conditions, building specifications, final equipment selection, negotiated rates, and applicable taxes (GST 18%). This tool should be used for preliminary budgeting only. A detailed bill of quantities (BOQ) is recommended for final tendering.
